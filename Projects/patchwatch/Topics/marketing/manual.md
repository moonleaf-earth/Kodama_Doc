# Manual Marketing Tasks (Minimized)

> Only tasks that genuinely require a human. Keep this list short.
> Everything else should be automated or delegated to an AI agent.

---

## One-Time Setup (Do Once)

### 1. Approve and Publish First Blog Post
- Review Claude's first generated blog draft (`/blog-drafts/`)
- Approve or edit, then merge to main (triggers auto-deploy)
- **Why manual:** First post sets tone; worth a human read

### 2. Sign Up for Tool Accounts
- ConvertKit (or Resend sequences), Buffer, Apollo/Hunter, Plausible
- **Why manual:** Account creation requires identity verification

### 3. Seed NGO Partnerships (5-10 orgs)
- Send a personal note to 5-10 specific NGO contacts offering free Pro access
- Can be email — no call required
- **Why manual:** First relationships need a human touch; sets referral/testimonial base
- **Minimize:** Claude drafts the email; you send it

### 4. Product Hunt — Day-Of Engagement
- Be available for ~2 hours on PH launch day to respond to comments
- **Why manual:** Community expects founder presence; responses can't be fully automated
- **Minimize:** Claude pre-writes response templates for likely questions

---

## Recurring (Monthly)

### 5. Review Marketing Weekly Report
- Claude/Coworker generates the report; you spend 10 minutes reading it
- Approve any budget changes or strategy pivots
- **Why manual:** Decision-making on spend or channel shifts

### 6. Approve Social Post Queue
- Review weekly batch of 10 social posts before Buffer queues them
- Takes ~5 minutes; reject/edit duds
- **Why manual:** Brand voice QA; avoids AI saying something off

---

## As-Needed

### 7. Respond to Inbound Press Inquiries
- If a journalist replies to a pitch, respond personally
- Claude drafts the reply; you send it
- **Why manual:** Journalists notice when replies feel bot-generated

### 8. Accept/Reject New NGO Partnership Requests
- As inbound interest grows, triage new org requests
- **Why manual:** Judgment call on org credibility and fit

---

## Explicitly NOT manual (fully automated)

- Blog post writing → Claude Coworker
- Social post drafting → Claude Coworker
- Email sequence sending → Resend / ConvertKit
- Analytics reporting → Claude Coworker
- News monitoring → Gemini / n8n RSS
- NGO list building → AI research tasks
- Image generation → DALL-E
